About Me

Kadeeja Abdul Gafoor

Digital Marketing Executive, Web Developing Student

Hello there! I'm Kadeeja Abdul Gafoor, and I'm thrilled to welcome you to my corner of the web. Here's a little Glimpse into who I am.

I am a Digital Marketing Executive and Web Developing student based in Calicut, Kerala. I persuaded BCom degree and a diploma in digital marketing. With a passion for technology and developing, I love to explore more and learn about the Digital World around us. By day, you'll find me as a Digital Marketing Executive, where I will be responsible for implementing effective digital marketing strategies, driving online brand awareness, and executing campaigns that generate leads and contribute to the overall growth of the organization. By night, I transform into a Web Developing Student, where I learn about the Web development.

My journey to the digital world began from 2018 where I realizes the future depends on technology an IT. Since then, I've had the privilege to increase my knowledge on digital marketing and now learning about web development. Every day I strive to learn particular topics on Web Development and complete the tasks of being a Digital Marketing Executive.
